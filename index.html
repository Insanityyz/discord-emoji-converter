How to Enable Developer Mode on Discord

On Mobile:

  1. Open the Discord app on your mobile device.
  2. Tap on your profile picture in the bottom right corner to open User Settings.
  3. Scroll down and select Advanced.
  4. Toggle the Developer Mode switch to enable it.

On PC:

  1. Open Discord on your PC and log in.
  2. Click on the gear icon (User Settings) at the bottom left corner next to your username.
  3. Scroll down and select Advanced in the left sidebar.
  4. Toggle the Developer Mode switch to enable it.

How to Get Emoji IDs on Discord

On Mobile:

  1. Open the Discord app and navigate to a server where you can see the emoji you want to get the ID for.
  2. Tap and hold on the emoji to bring up a menu.
  3. Select Copy Emoji ID from the menu. (Ensure Developer Mode is enabled as described above.)

On PC:

  1. Open Discord and go to a server where the emoji is used.
  2. Click on the emoji to open the emoji picker.
  3. Right-click on the emoji (or click on the three dots if visible) and select Copy ID. (Ensure Developer Mode is enabled as described above.)
